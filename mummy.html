    • A Tale of Mummification

    Everyone has their own way of remembering those they’ve lost. Some hold their memories deep within their hearts, while others choose to let go and move on. No matter the approach, every effort is worth it. In this project, we use an infographic that combines storytelling with a journey through the lost art of ancient empires to illustrate the process of mummification, conveying this deeper meaning. The audience steps into the shoes of a husband who has just lost his beloved wife to an ancient curse and navigates through enigmatic rooms, each representing a crucial step in the mummification process. Once you’ve completed the journey, you’ll grasp the true meaning behind your character’s actions.
