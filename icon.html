    • The Choosen

    Most people have heard of Christ and His miracle stories. In paintings, He is often depicted with His disciples, particularly the 12 apostles He personally chose. These 12 Apostles were the closest disciples of Jesus, selected by Him to spread His teachings and continue His mission after His martyrdom. This project illustrates the stories behind these 12 special individuals, each with their own unique and significant story that made them the chosen ones. In each icon, we strive to convey not only their well-known stories but also the myths associated with them and their lasting legacy. These are 12 icons, each with 3 adaptation sizes.
