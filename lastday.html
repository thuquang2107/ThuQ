    • The Last Day On Earth

    Group project/ collaboration with Bao Chau, Phuong Bach, and Harlee

    What if an apocalypse were to occur? Can we find a way to survive, or should we simply accept our fate? A 3 days 3 nights experience themed around the past, present, and future, offers an incredible opportunity for people to engage with each other, share their experiences, and reflect on how they can apply the lessons learned to live more fully in the present moment as if it is the last day on Earth. By offering a diverse range of activities, entertainment, and cuisine, we ensure that each guest has a memorable and transformative experience worth every dollar of their ticket.
