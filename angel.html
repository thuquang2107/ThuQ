    • The Angel

    Individual project

    There is a Vietnamese saying that I have heard for years: ”Adults were once children, but children have never been adults. Adults see the problems of a 17-year-old as just the mouth of a well, but for that child, it is an entire sky.". That statement, unsurprisingly, resonates with my experience of being in my 20s. In my 20s, I find myself tragically gripped by a fear- named “maturity”. From a child's perspective, this fear is understandable. But that is something unavoidable. One day this child will be a adult. Instead of scaring, the best way to overcome a fear is to fight it. Therefore, I consider this project a great chance to encourage myself to accept what can come and what can not change via the hidden meaning of the poem “The Angel” written by William Blake.
